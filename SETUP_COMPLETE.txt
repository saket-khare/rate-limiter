Redis = Remote Dictionary Server

It's an in-memory database (super fast!) that stores key-value pairs.

Example:
  Key: "rate_limit:fixed:192.168.1.1"
  Value: 7 (number of requests)
  TTL: 45 seconds (auto-expires)

Perfect for rate limiting because:
âœ… Extremely fast (< 1ms operations)
âœ… Atomic operations (no race conditions)
âœ… TTL support (auto-cleanup)
âœ… Works across multiple servers
